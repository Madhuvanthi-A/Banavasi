CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY & VOLUNTEERING

Corporate Social Responsibility: -

It is estimated that there is about 20,000 Crores of CSR money available in India. Most of it is being spent in the urban places and there is very little that is reaching the grass root society or villages.

Volunteering: -

Corporate and their employees need a space to volunteer. There is a need for meaningful engagement by companies and their employees. We see that doctors/ scientists are sweeping the roads trying to volunteer their time and services. Is this the best they can do with their expertise and time?

Solutions: -

CSR money has to move into villages to address more serious problems like poverty eradication, organic production of food stuff, upgrading hospitals, upgrading schools, bringing technology in farming, reforestation, working with local NGO’s etc.

Creating a platform for the urban corporate to meet the rural world is the need of the hour.

Adopting a village or a cause would be the way forward. This will ensure that the employees get an in-depth understanding of themselves and the world around is important. The CSR money can flow directly / thru and NGO and address local issues.

GDSET.ORG have been catering to this segment and we are the bridge between the rural and urban cultures. We have done this easily, as we as an organization understand both the need of the volunteer / company and at the same time their need to explore the local area (both for work and fun). There should be a good mix of travel and social cause to keep them engaged in the long run.

GDSET.ORG has been instrumental in getting companies / schools and NGO’s enlisted with us to do their CSR activity. Once we show the success of such models, we can have such models all over India.
